Popular Destinations We Visit

Masai Mara National Reserve

Famous for the Great Migration and home to all Big Five animals. Best visited between July and October.

Big Five Year-round Great Migration

Amboseli National Park

Best place to see elephants with Mount Kilimanjaro as backdrop. Amazing photography opportunities.

Mt. Kilimanjaro Large Elephants Photography

Tsavo National Parks

Kenya's largest wilderness area, famous for red elephants and diverse wildlife. Great for adventurous spirits.

Wilderness Red Elephants Adventure

Lake Nakuru National Park

Famous for millions of flamingos and home to rhinos. A birdwatcher's paradise with over 400 bird species.

Flamingos Rhino Sanctuary Birdwatching
