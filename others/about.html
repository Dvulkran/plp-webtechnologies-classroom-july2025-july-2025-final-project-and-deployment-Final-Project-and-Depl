About Safari Adventures Kenya

Born from a passion for Kenya's incredible wildlife and rich culture

Our Story

Safari Adventures Kenya was founded with a simple mission: to share the breathtaking beauty and incredible wildlife of our homeland with visitors from around the world. Based in Chuka, Tharaka-Nithi County, we have deep roots in this beautiful country and an intimate knowledge of its hidden treasures.

Our journey began when we realized that many tourists were missing out on the authentic Kenya experience. We wanted to create something different - safaris that go beyond the typical tourist routes and offer genuine encounters with both wildlife and local communities.

Today, we're proud to be one of Kenya's most trusted safari operators, having helped hundreds of travelers create unforgettable memories while supporting local conservation efforts and communities.

What We Stand For

Conservation

We're committed to protecting Kenya's wildlife and natural habitats through responsible tourism practices and supporting local conservation initiatives.

Community

Our tours directly benefit local communities by providing employment opportunities and supporting local businesses and cultural preservation.

Authenticity

We offer genuine experiences that showcase the real Kenya - its wildlife, cultures, and landscapes in their most natural form.

Excellence

From planning to execution, we maintain the highest standards of service to ensure every safari exceeds your expectations.
